Spicy Sweet Chilli Chicken on Rice!

Recipe!

  • 2 or 3 chicken breasts
  • 2 x egg
  • 1 tbs smoked paprika
  • 1 tbs garlic powder
  • 1 tbs flour

Combine eggs, seasoning, and flour in a bowl and roll chicken until coated. Then fry chicken in around 1/2 cup oil until crispy.

Once fried:

  • 1/4 cup sweet chili sauce
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tbs of white vinegar
  • 1 tsp fresh garlic

Combine then add to the pan with crispy chicken, and cook on low heat until the sauce has all fried away and gone onto the chicken (there should be a thick coating on the chicken).

Rice!

  • Wash your rice
  • Into a pot add 1 cup of rice to 2 cups of water (I add chicken stock to mine!)
  • Add one clove of garlic to water (optional)
  • Allow to boil then turn down to low heat until all the water is evaporated, try and add more water if necessary
